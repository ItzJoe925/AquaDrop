How AquaDrop Works

AquaDrop places 24 acoustic micro-sensors throughout your data center's sub-floor plenum and overhead cooling infrastructure. Each sensor continuously listens for the characteristic high-frequency noise signature that escaping pressurized coolant produces — a sound pattern no normal chilled water flow creates.

01
Sense
Sensors detect acoustic anomalies in chilled water, coolant, and fire suppression piping every 10 seconds.
02
Analyze
AI compares the sound pattern against thousands of known leak and pressure-drop signatures, computing a probability score.
03
Locate
Time-difference-of-arrival (TDOA) math triangulates exactly which pipe section is leaking — down to 3 meters in the sub-floor plenum.
04
Alert
Your facilities team receives an alert with the precise location, preventing coolant loss, server downtime, and costly emergency shutdowns.
